My Car Speaker System

This video is a showcase of what subwoofer I have in my 2005 Honda Cr-V. It is an Alpine W12S4 subwoofer hooked up to a 600W JBL amplifier. I also have a Pioneer AVIC5201-NEX aftermarket Radio. Easily reaching around $1500 for the total price I have spent on this system. I plan to use this car for a few more years or until it stop running. I love music and the good quality sound I have purchased.
